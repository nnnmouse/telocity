== Quick Reference Cheat Sheet for -x, --regex in batchgen (telocity bg).

[cols="2,2,3", options="header"]
|===
| Use Case | Pattern | Example Match

| Chinese Chapters (Arabic)
| `^第\d+章`
| `第15章`

| Chinese Chapters (Mixed)
| `^第[0-9一二三四五六七八九十百千零]+章`
| `第十五章` or `第15章`

| Standard English Chapters
| `^Chapter \d+`
| `Chapter 14`

| English (Colons & Mixed Casing)
| `^[Cc]hapter\s+\d+:`
| `chapter 105:` or `Chapter 1:`

| Markdown Section Headers
| `^##`
| `## Section Title`
|===
